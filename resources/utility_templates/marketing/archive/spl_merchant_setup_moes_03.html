How it works

Order Presentment:

All Splick–it mobile and online orders will be presented at your store via a Splick–it dedicated ticket printer, which will be supplied to you at no additional cost.

Pricing:

Just 2% of orders processed through Splick–it mobile and online ordering. Additionally, the cost of credit card processing fees will pass through at a rate of 2.69% + $0.25 per Splick–it transaction.
