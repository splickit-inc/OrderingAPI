How it works

Order Presentment:

All Splick–it mobile and online orders will be delivered to your store via fax.

Pricing:

We offer two pricing options. Both prices include our mobile and online ordering services. You will select your desired option in the Splick–it Merchant Portal.

  • Pay as you go: 5% of orders processed through Splick–it mobile and online ordering (plus credit card processing fees)
  • Pay once: $599/yr. (plus credit card processing fees)

Do you already use Mercury Payments?

If you currently have a Mercury Payments account, we can provide you with the same credit card processing rates that you already pay.*

*This only applies to the $599 annual plan.
