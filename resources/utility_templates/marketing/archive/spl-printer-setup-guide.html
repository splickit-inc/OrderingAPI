Printer Setup Step by Step Guide:

  1. Remove printer from box and identify the printer, power cord and instructions.
  2. Plug power cord into back of the printer set near tortilla press.
  3. Plug power cord into a nearby outlet. (Internet is NOT required)
  4. Turn power switch located on the back of the printer to the "ON" position.
  5. Once it has fully powered on, the printer should automatically print your store address and phone number. Please confirm that all of this information is correct! If it is not correct, please contact us immediately at 303.481.3656.
  6. If the information printed is correct, you should receive a test order from Splickit sometime later in the day.
  7. If the test order is successfully received and your Store Profile has been updated in the Merchant Portal, then your location will be activated in the next 24 hours. You will receive a confirmation email once your store has been successfully activated.
